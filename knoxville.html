Knoxville, Tennessee. A blend of Appalachian heritage and vibrant university life.

Knoxville's Population:

Knoxville's population has 190,740 residents, making it the largest city in the East Tennessee Grand Division and the state's third-most-populous city after Nashville and Memphis.

When was Knoxville incorporated?

Knoxville, Tennessee, was officially incorporated as a city on October 27, 1815. This marked an important milestone in its history

Which region in Tennessee is Knoxville located?

Knoxville is located in East Tennessee, which is the eastern region of the state of Tennessee, United States.

Knoxville's Classification

Knoxville is classified as a mid-sized city in Tennessee. It serves as a hub for East Tennessee, featuring a mix of urban and suburban areas. The city is known for its historical significance, cultural attractions, and its role as a center for education, healthcare, and commerce in the region.
